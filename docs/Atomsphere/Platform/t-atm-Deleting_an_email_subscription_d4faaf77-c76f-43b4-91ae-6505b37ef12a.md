You can delete email subscriptions.

## Procedure

1. Select **Settings** \> **User Information**, the **Email Alerts** tab, and the **Integration** tab.

2. In the subscriptions list, click the subscriptionâ€™s **![X or Delete Subscription icon](./Images/main-ic-x-red-stylized-16_5cfc1d0e-0ef7-44cc-bacf-4b4116afca79.jpg) Delete Subscription** icon.

3. In the confirmation dialog, click **OK**.
